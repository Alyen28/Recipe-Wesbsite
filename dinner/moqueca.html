Moqueca

Delicious Brazilian fish stew with tilapia (Moqueca)!

Ingredients

Original recipe yelds 6 servings

  • 3 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 Â½ pounds tilapia fillets, cut into chunks
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 4 large bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 (16 ounce) can diced tomatoes, drained
  • 1 (16 ounce) can coconut milk

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Whisk lime juice, cumin, paprika, garlic, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl.
  3. Add tilapia and toss to evenly coat. Cover the bowl and marinate in the refrigerator for least 20 minutes, up to 24 hours.
  4. Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat.
  5. Add onions; cook and stir 1 to 2 minutes. Reduce heat to medium.
  6. Add bell peppers, tilapia, and diced tomatoes in succeeding layers; pour in coconut milk.
  7. Cover the pot; simmer 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  8. Continue cooking until tilapia completely cooked through, 5 to 10 minutes more.
  9. Serve and enjoy!
