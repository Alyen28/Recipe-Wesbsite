Feijoada

Delicious brazilian pork and black bean stew (Feijoada)!

Ingredients

Original recipe yelds 4 servings

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 (12 ounce) pork tenderloin, cut into ½-inch cubes
  • 1 (19 ounce) can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • ¼ cup water
  • 3 chorizo sausages, cut into ½-inch thick pieces
  • 1 ½ cups chicken stock
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add onion and garlic; cook until onion softens and turns translucent; transfer onion to a saucepan
  4. Pour the remaining tablespoon vegetable oil into the skillet; place over medium-high heat. Add cubed pork; cook until well browned.
  5. Meanwhile, add ¾ black beans and water to a blender; pulse until finely chopped, but not quite smooth.
  6. Transfer bean purée to the saucepan; stir in remaining whole black beans, pork cubes, chorizo and chicken stock.
  7. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, then reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer 30 minutes.
  8. Season with salt and black pepper.
  9. Serve and enjoy!
