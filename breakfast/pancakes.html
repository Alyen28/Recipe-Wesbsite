Pancakes

Good old-fashioned pancakes!

Ingredients

Original recipe yelds 8 servings

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt, or more to taste
  • 1 ¼ cups milk
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 large egg

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Sift flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt together in a large bowl.
  3. Make a well in the center and add milk, melted butter, and egg; mix until smooth.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake;
  6. Cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, about 2 to 3 minutes.
  7. Flip and cook until browned on the other side. Repeat with remaining batter.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
