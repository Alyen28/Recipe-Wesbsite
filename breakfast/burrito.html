Breakfast Burritos

A large batch of mexican-inspired breakfast burritos!

Ingredients

Original recipe yelds 20 servings

  • 12 large eggs
  • ⅔ cup milk
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 pound bulk pork sausage
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • ½ red onion, diced
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 (1 ounce) package taco seasoning
  • 1 ½ cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 20 (6 inch) flour tortillas

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Whisk together eggs, milk, and salt in a large bowl.
  3. Heat butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Pour in egg mixture; cook and stir until eggs are completely set, about 5 minutes.
  5. Break up cooked eggs into small pieces and place into a large bowl. Set aside.
  6. Heat the same large skillet over medium heat.
  7. Cook and stir sausage and garlic in the hot skillet for 5 minutes, then add onion.
  8. Continue to cook and stir until sausage is crumbly, evenly browned, and no longer pink.
  9. Drain and discard any excess grease.
  10. Transfer sausage to eggs in the bowl.
  11. Stir in tomato, cilantro and taco seasoning until well combined.
  12. Allow mixture to cool to room temperature, then stir in Cheddar cheese.
  13. Lay a tortilla onto your work surface, then place some of the eggs on it.
  14. Fold the tortilla making a burrito shape and repeat the process with remaining ingredients.
  15. Wrap individual burritos tightly with plastic wrap and store in the freezer until ready to serve.
  16. Heat burritos in the microwave until hot, 3 to 4 minutes.
  17. Serve and enjoy!
