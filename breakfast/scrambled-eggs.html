Scrambled Eggs

Light and fluffy baked scrambled eggs!

Ingredients

Original recipe yelds 12 servings

  • ½ cup butter, melted
  • 24 large eggs
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons salt
  • 2 ½ cups milk

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  3. Pour melted butter into a 9x13-inch glass baking dish.
  4. Whisk eggs and salt together in a large bowl until well blended.
  5. Gradually whisk in milk. Pour egg mixture into the buttered dish.
  6. Bake uncovered in the preheated oven for 10 to 15 minutes.
  7. Stir egg mixture and continue to bake until eggs are set, 10 to 15 minutes more.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
