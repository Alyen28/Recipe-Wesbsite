Cuscuz

Cuscuz (couscous) recipe from the Northeast Region of Brazil!

Ingredients

Original recipe yelds 4-6 servings

  • 250 g pre cooked flaked corn flour or pre cooked corn flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon manioc starch
  • Approximately 400 ml water

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Mix the corn flour, manioc starch and salt, together, in a bowl.
  3. Gradually add the water and mix well until all ingredients are moist and mixture has a crumbly texture.
  4. Fill the steamer pot half full of water.
  5. Place the couscous mixture into the steamer basket and gently level the top without pressing.
  6. The mixture should remain soft to allow the steam to penetrate just right.
  7. Cover and cook over low heat for about 15 minutes, until it begins to release its aroma.
  8. Add butter or milk, if you like. It is a very versatile side dish to vegetables, meat, fish, eggs, cheese, coconut and sweet courses.
  9. Cut into slices, serve hot and enjoy!
