Tapioca

Brazilian crepe-like pancakes made with tapioca flour!

Ingredients

Original recipe yelds 2-4 servings

  • 1 cup tapioca starch, more as needed
  • 1/2 cup water, approximately
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Add the tapioca starch to a medium bowl.
  3. Gradually add the water, 2 tablespoons at a time, stirring with your fingers as you go.
  4. The mixture will form clumps; use your finger to break them apart.
  5. Keep stirring and adding water until the entire mixture has formed medium-to-small clumps.
  6. If your mixture is too moist, add a little more starch until you find a good balance.
  7. Pass the moistened starch through a very fine sieve into a clean bowl.
  8. Use a wooden spoon to vigorously stir the starch in the sieve to help it pass through.
  9. Once sieved, add the salt, and stir again.
  10. Heat a nonstick pan over medium heat.
  11. Working quickly, evenly sprinkle the sifted starch into a thin layer until all the pan is covered.
  12. Using the back of a spoon, even out the crepe and cook it for about 30 seconds.
  13. Flip the crepe over with a spatula. Cook for an additional 30 to 40 seconds.
  14. Pass crepe to a plate. Repeat process until you've used all the mixture.
  15. Fill the tapioca with butter, if you like, or any fillings of your choice.
  16. Serve and enjoy!
