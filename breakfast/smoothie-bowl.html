Smoothie Bowl

Quick and easy blueberry smoothie bowl!

Ingredients

Original recipe yelds 1 serving

  • 1 cup frozen blueberries
  • ½ banana
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 tablespoon cashew butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ banana, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon sliced almonds
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened shredded coconut

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Blend blueberries, 1/2 banana, water, cashew butter, and vanilla extract together in a blender until smooth
  3. Pour into a medium bowl.
  4. Top smoothie with sliced banana, almonds, and coconut
  5. Enjoy!
