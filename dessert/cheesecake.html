Cheesecake

Creamy cheesecake with cherry topping!

Ingredients

Original recipe yelds 16 servings

  • 1 ¾ cups Honey Maid Graham Cracker Crumbs
  • ⅓ cup butter, melted
  • 1 ¼ cups sugar, divided
  • 3 (8 ounce) packages Philadelphia Cream Cheese, softened
  • 1 cup Breakstone's or Knudsen Sour Cream
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
  3. Mix graham crumbs, butter, and 1/4 cup sugar together in a large bowl.
  4. Press crumbs onto bottom of 9-inch springform pan.
  5. Beat cream cheese and remaining sugar in large bowl with mixer until blended.
  6. Add sour cream and vanilla; mix well.
  7. Add eggs, 1 at a time, beating on low speed after each addition just until blended.
  8. Pour mixture over crust.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven until the center is almost set, about 1 hour to 1 hour 10 minutes.
  10. Run knife around rim of pan to loosen cake; cool before removing rim.
  11. Refrigerate the cheesecake for 4 hours.
  12. Top with pie filling.
  13. Serve and enjoy!
