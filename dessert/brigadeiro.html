Brigadeiro

A classic Brazilian treat similar to chocolate truffles!

Ingredients

Original recipe yelds 20 servings

  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Chocolate sprinkles for rolling

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Combine condensed milk, cocoa, and butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Cook and stir until thickened, about 10 minutes.
  4. Transfer mixture into a shallow bowl and allow to cool.
  5. Pinch off some of the dough and roll into a small ball; place on a plate.
  6. Repeat with remaining mixture, then roll each brigadeiro in chocolate sprinkles.
  7. Chill brigadeiros until ready to serve and enjoy!
